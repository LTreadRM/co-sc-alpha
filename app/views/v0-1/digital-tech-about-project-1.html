About the project

Is this a digital or technology project?

A digital project is defined as any external facing service delivered through the internet to citizens, businesses, civil society or non-government organisations. This includes, but is not limited to information services, websites, transactional services, web applications, mobile apps, and extranets.

A technology project is defined as any internal facing service, delivered Government to Government.

Describe the technology you are requesting money for

Be as specific as you can at this stage. Your Senior Technology Advisor can advise you on what information they would expect to see, based on the circumstances of your proposed spend.

How will the proposed technology provide value for money?

You should include whether the technology is already in use in your Department, or can be reused elsewhere.
