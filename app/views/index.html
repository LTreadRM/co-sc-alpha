Spend Control alpha prototype

This contains versions of the spend control prototype for alpha phase.

Note that versions are in reverse date order (most recent is at the top).

Version 1.3 (January 2022)

This is a mid-fidelity mock-up of changes stemming from results of testing version 1.2 and the following service assessment.

  • Usability test. GOV.UK page
  • Department view: Beginning a case
  • Cabinet Office - pipeline view
  • Cabinet Office view: Specifying conditions
  • Department view: Responding to conditions & communications from Cabinet Office
  • Cabinet Office view: Responding to Departments' responses

Version 1.2 (9 November 2021)

This is the first Heroku-based prototype for detailed usability/summative testing with participants. This includes a mini-iteration of the departmental prototype after some fairly catastrophic failures after the first few sessions.

  • Cabinet Office - pipeline view
  • Cabinet Office - Conditions
  • Departments - begin case and conditions
  • Tree structure prototype - mid-fidelity wireframes of above

Version 1.1 (8 and 10 October 2021)

This is the second mid-fidelty prototype for usability/summative testing and exploration of themes with participants.

  • Conditions management (Test 2)
  • Dashboard
  • Tree structure 2
